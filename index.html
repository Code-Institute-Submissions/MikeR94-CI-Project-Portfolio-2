Rules

There are 3 difficulty levels to choose from. Picking Easy will give 10 points per correct question, Medium 20 points and Hard 30 points. You will always have 30 seconds to answer and each difficulty level will have 8 questions. Your time remaining will be added to your score as points so it's worth to try and answer fast!

If you answer a question correctly, a planet         will light up but if you answer incorrectly or don't answer then the planet will remain greyed out. 

You can toggle the sound on to hear game sounds or toggle it off if you don't want to hear them. Please note that clicking the button will reset your settings and default the sound back to on.

When you have answered a question, this icon will pop up. Click this to go to the next question. This will automatically pop up if you fail to answer within the time.
